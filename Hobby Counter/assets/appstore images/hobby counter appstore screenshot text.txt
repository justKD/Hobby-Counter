Simple, clean, easy to use.
Perfect for board games, knitting,
and any other hobby!

Customize each individual counter.
Set the title, subtitle, min, max, color.
let it wrap count or link it to the next counter.

Easy navigation.
Just swipe left and right on each
individual counter to navigate menus.

Save and load projects.
Rename, delete, and reorder
to keep things organized.

Every project includes a notebook.
Jot down instructions, supplies, how often Ben cheats.
Anything goes.
